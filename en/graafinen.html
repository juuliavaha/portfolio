Graphic Design

Design and implementation of the game world and characters


The assignment was to design the visual appearance of a game aimed at primary school-aged children. The game features two characters: a small and wise Flea and a strong Bull. The goal of the game is to develop the Flea’s wisdom and the Bull’s strength by answering mathematical questions correctly. For each correct answer, the Flea gains more weight as part of “training,” and as the weight increases, the player progresses through different levels. The game concept itself was provided as part of the assignment.

When designing the characters, I aimed to emphasize their personalities: the Flea is small, intelligent, and energetic, equipped with glasses and a book/notebook, while the Bull is muscular, slightly clueless, and friendly. The colors and game elements were designed to be vibrant and easy for children to understand.


The work was created using Adobe Illustrator, which was a new tool for me, and I learned to use its features through challenges during the process. I aimed to make the characters clear, colorful, and simple, with a cartoon-like style. During the project, I also improved my computer drawing skills, as I only had a mouse available instead of a drawing tablet.

Overall, the project was an inspiring and educational experience that strengthened my interest in game development and digital illustration.
