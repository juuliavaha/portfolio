About



Hi! I'm Juulia Vähä and I'm studying Business Information Technology at South Eastern Finland University of Applied Sciences in my final year. I currently work as a supervisor in a retail field, but Im now looking for new job opportunities in the Pirkanmaa region. My biggest passion is working on projects where I can see my work come to life and where I can use my problem-solving and visual skills. Im especially interested in coding assignments, software development and UI/UX design. Im fascinated by new technologies and I want to develop my skills to a top level.


What can you expect from me?

I want to learn new things constantly, which is why I think the field of Business Information Technology is so fascinating - learning never ends. I enjoy versatile projects that give me the opportunity to improve my skills. In my life, I always have at least one project to work on, because I'm a person who doesn't want to stand still for too long. When I face an interesting project, I cannot stop myself from working on it, and I want to make it the best version possbile. So, I work diligently, faithfully, and with ambition.

I love people, and I take care of my relationships both in my personal and professional life. A good work environment and cooperation with co-workers and customers mean a lot to me, and those things are a huge part of well-being at work. I believe that open communication, mutual trust, and a strong team spirit positively affect work results and customer experience.

When I'm not sitting at the computer, I'm usually crocheting or out for a run. Seeing my work come to life, doing meticulous work, and challenging and developing myself gives me energy in my everyday life.

If you think that I would be a good fit for your team as a person and as an employee, please feel free to contact me so we can discuss further. 😊
